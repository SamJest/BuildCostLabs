What can change the total

Dimensions, waste, coverage assumptions, site access, and product choice usually move the total the most.

When to remeasure

Remeasure when the layout is irregular, the substrate is poor, or the supplier pack size does not match the default assumptions.

How many packs of flooring do I need?

Calculate area, add waste, and divide by pack coverage, then round up.

Is extra flooring worth buying?

Usually yes, because matching future repairs can be difficult.
