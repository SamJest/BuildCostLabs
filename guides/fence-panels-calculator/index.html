What can change the total

Dimensions, waste, coverage assumptions, site access, and product choice usually move the total the most.

When to remeasure

Remeasure when the layout is irregular, the substrate is poor, or the supplier pack size does not match the default assumptions.

How many fence panels do I need?

Divide the total run by the panel width, round appropriately, and then check posts and end conditions.

Should fence estimates include concrete?

Usually yes, because post concrete is a material cost many quick fence estimates forget.
